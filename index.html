About Me

Passionate about the intersection of mathematics,poetry, technology, and creative expression

I'm a Mathematics and Computer Science student at Maseno University, expressing the beauty of human experience through poetry, driven by curiosity and a passion for understanding how technology can solve real-world problems.

My Journey

My academic journey at Maseno University has been a fascinating blend of analytical thinking and creative expression. While diving deep into mathematical concepts and programming languages, I've discovered that poetry provides a unique lens through which to view and interpret the world.

This dual perspective has shaped my approach to problem-solving, allowing me to think both logically and creatively. Whether I'm debugging code or crafting verses, I'm always seeking patterns, beauty, and meaning.

Global Perspective

Living in Kenya has given me a unique vantage point on global trends and developments. I'm particularly interested in how technology is transforming African societies and how local innovations can have global impact.
